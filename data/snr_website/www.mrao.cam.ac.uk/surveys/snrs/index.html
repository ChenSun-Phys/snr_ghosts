The documentation and the detailed listings include links to abstracts/papers for almost all references at "The NASA Astrophysics Data System" (ADS).

Please cite the following paper for the summary data from this catalogue:

  • Green D. A., 2019, Journal of Astrophysics and Astronomy, 40, 36.
If you make use of this version of the catalogue, then please also cite:
  • Green D. A., 2019, `A Catalogue of Galactic Supernova Remnants (2019 June version)', Cavendish Laboratory, Cambridge, United Kingdom (available at "http://www.mrao.cam.ac.uk/surveys/snrs/").
The summary data are also available from VizieR, as catalogue VII/284 , where objects can be selected on the basis of their parameters (e.g. coordinates, angular size, flux density, type, etc.).
