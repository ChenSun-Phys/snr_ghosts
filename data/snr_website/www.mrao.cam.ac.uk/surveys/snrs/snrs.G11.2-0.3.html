G11.2−0.3

  • Right Ascension: 18 11 27
  • Declination: −19 25
  • Size (/arcmin): 4
  • Type: C
  • Flux density at 1 GHz (/Jy): 22
  • Spectral Index: 0.5

Notes: Probably associated with the SN of AD386.

Radio: Symmetrical clumpy shell, with flatter spectrum core.
X-ray: Shell, with hard spectrum centrally brightened region around pulsar.
Point sources: Central pulsar.
Distance: HI absorption indicates 4.4 kpc.
