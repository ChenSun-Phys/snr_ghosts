G18.8+0.3

Kes 67

  • Right Ascension: 18 23 58
  • Declination: −12 23
  • Size (/arcmin): 17×11
  • Type: S
  • Flux density at 1 GHz (/Jy): 33
  • Spectral Index: 0.46

Notes: Has been called G18.9+0.3.

Radio: Incomplete shell, in complex region near the HII region W39.
Distance: Association with molecular cloud suggests 12 kpc, and HI absorption suggests 13.8 kpc.
