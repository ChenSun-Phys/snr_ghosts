G18.9−1.1

  • Right Ascension: 18 29 50
  • Declination: −12 58
  • Size (/arcmin): 33
  • Type: C?
  • Flux density at 1 GHz (/Jy): 37
  • Spectral Index: 0.39

Notes: Has been called G18.95−1.1 and G18.94−1.04.

Radio: Non-thermal, diffuse partially limb-brightened, with central ridge.
Optical: Detected.
X-ray: Partial shell.
Point sources: Compact X-ray source, with diffuse nebula.
Distance: Optical absorption suggests 1.8 kpc.
