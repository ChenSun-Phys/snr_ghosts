G205.5+0.5

Monoceros Nebula

  • Right Ascension: 06 39 00
  • Declination: +06 30
  • Size (/arcmin): 220
  • Type: S
  • Flux density at 1 GHz (/Jy): 140
  • Spectral Index: 0.4
Radio: In complex region, parts may be HII regions.
Optical: Large ring, near Rosette nebula.
X-ray: Possibly detected.
Distance: Association with molecular cloud suggests 1.6 to 2.0 kpc.
