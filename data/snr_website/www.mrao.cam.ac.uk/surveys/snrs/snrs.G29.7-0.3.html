G29.7−0.3

Kes 75

  • Right Ascension: 18 46 25
  • Declination: −02 59
  • Size (/arcmin): 3
  • Type: C
  • Flux density at 1 GHz (/Jy): 10
  • Spectral Index: 0.63

Notes: Has erroneously been called G29.6+0.1.

Radio: Shell with flatter spectrum emission from centre.
X-ray: Thermal shell and non-thermal core, and central pulsar.
Point sources: X-ray pulsar.
Distance: Association with CO implies 11 kpc, and HI absorption suggests 5.6 kpc.
