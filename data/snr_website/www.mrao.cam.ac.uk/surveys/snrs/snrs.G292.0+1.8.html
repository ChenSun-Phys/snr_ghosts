G292.0+1.8

MSH 11−54

  • Right Ascension: 11 24 36
  • Declination: −59 16
  • Size (/arcmin): 12×8
  • Type: C
  • Flux density at 1 GHz (/Jy): 15
  • Spectral Index: 0.4
Radio: Centrally brightened source surrounded by a plateau of faint emission.
Optical: Oxygen rich.
X-ray: Ring of emission, with diffuse central nebula and pulsar.
Point sources: Central pulsar.
Distance: HI absorption implies 6.0 kpc.
