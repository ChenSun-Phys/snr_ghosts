G296.5+10.0

PKS 1209−51/52

  • Right Ascension: 12 09 40
  • Declination: −52 25
  • Size (/arcmin): 90×65
  • Type: S
  • Flux density at 1 GHz (/Jy): 48
  • Spectral Index: 0.5

Notes: Has been called G296.5+9.7, and erroneously G295.5+9.7.

Radio: Shell with two bright limbs.
Optical: Detected.
X-ray: Incomplete shell, with central pulsar.
Point sources: Central pulsar.
