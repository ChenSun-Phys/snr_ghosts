Radio: Shell, brightest to the S.
Optical: Filaments correspond well to the radio shell, brightest in SE.
X-ray: Brightest to NW, with point source near centre.
Point sources: Central, variable X-ray source, and nearby pulsar.
Distance: HI absorption indicates 3.1 kpc.
