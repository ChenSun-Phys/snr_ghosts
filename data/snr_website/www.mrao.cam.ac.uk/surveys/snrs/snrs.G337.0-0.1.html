G337.0−0.1

(CTB 33)

  • Right Ascension: 16 35 57
  • Declination: −47 36
  • Size (/arcmin): 1.5
  • Type: S
  • Flux density at 1 GHz (/Jy): 1.5
  • Spectral Index: 0.6?

Notes: This entry refers to a small (1.'5) SNR, not the larger previously catalogued G337.0−0.1. Has mistakenly been called G337.7−0.1.

Radio: Shell, in a complex region.
Point sources: Associated with a soft gamma repeater.
Distance: Association with CTB 33 gives 11 kpc.
