G34.7−0.4

W44, 3C392

  • Right Ascension: 18 56 00
  • Declination: +01 22
  • Size (/arcmin): 35×27
  • Type: C
  • Flux density at 1 GHz (/Jy): 240
  • Spectral Index: 0.37

Notes: Has been called G34.6−0.5.

Radio: Distorted shell, brighter to the E, with pulsar and associated nebula.
Optical: Diffuse emission.
X-ray: Centrally concentrated, thermal spectrum, plus pulsar wind nebula.
Point sources: Pulsar within the boundary of the remnant.
Distance: HI absorption suggests 3.0 kpc, and optical absorption suggests 2.1 kpc.
