G41.1−0.3

3C397

  • Right Ascension: 19 07 34
  • Declination: +07 08
  • Size (/arcmin): 4.5×2.5
  • Type: S
  • Flux density at 1 GHz (/Jy): 25
  • Spectral Index: 0.50
Radio: 3C397 is two sources: the E is the SNR, the W is a HII region.
X-ray: Brighter to the E and W, with central component.
Distance: HI absorption suggest 8.5 kpc.
