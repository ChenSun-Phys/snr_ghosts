G49.2−0.7

(W51)

  • Right Ascension: 19 23 50
  • Declination: +14 06
  • Size (/arcmin): 30
  • Type: S?
  • Flux density at 1 GHz (/Jy): 160?
  • Spectral Index: 0.3?

Notes: Has erroneously been called G49.1−0.1.

Radio: In complex region, parameters uncertain.
Optical: Some diffuse emission possibly associated.
X-ray: Elongated east–west.
Distance: Association with CO gives 6 kpc, optical absorption suggests 5.7 kpc, and HI absorption suggests 5.4 kpc.
