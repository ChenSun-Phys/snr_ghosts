G5.4−1.2

Milne 56

  • Right Ascension: 18 02 10
  • Declination: −24 54
  • Size (/arcmin): 35
  • Type: C?
  • Flux density at 1 GHz (/Jy): 35?
  • Spectral Index: 0.2?

Notes: Part been called G5.3−1.0. Has been suggested that this is not a SNR.

Radio: Incomplete shell, including wide `v' of emission to east with small flat-spectrum source at apex.
Optical: Detected.
X-ray: Pulsar detected, with faint extension.
Point sources: Pulsar nearby, in flat spectrum source.
Distance: HI absorption suggests >4.3 kpc.
