G54.4−0.3

(HC40)

  • Declination: +18 56
  • Size (/arcmin): 40
  • Type: S
  • Flux density at 1 GHz (/Jy): 28

Notes: Has been called G54.5−0.3.

Radio: Shell, in complex region.

Distance: HI and CO observations suggest 6.6 kpc.
