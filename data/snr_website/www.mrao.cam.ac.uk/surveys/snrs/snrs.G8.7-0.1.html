G8.7−0.1

(W30)

  • Right Ascension: 18 05 30
  • Declination: −21 26
  • Size (/arcmin): 45
  • Type: S?
  • Flux density at 1 GHz (/Jy): 80
  • Spectral Index: 0.5

Notes: Has been called G8.6−0.1.

Radio: Clumpy non-thermal shell, with low-frequency turnover.
X-ray: Northern edge detected.
Point sources: Pulsar inside western edge.
