G93.3+6.9

DA 530, 4C(T)55.38.1

  • Right Ascension: 20 52 25
  • Declination: +55 21
  • Size (/arcmin): 27×20
  • Type: C?
  • Flux density at 1 GHz (/Jy): 9
  • Spectral Index: 0.45

Notes: Has been called G93.2+6.7.

Radio: Shell, with two bright limbs, highly polarised.
X-ray: Compact central source.
Distance: HI observations suggest 2.2 kpc.
